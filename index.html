How We Work Together

Choose the model that fits your project size, budget, and working style.

📄

Fixed-Scope Project

Fully defined scope, deliverables & timeline with a fixed price agreed upfront. No surprises, no scope creep.

  • Clearly defined deliverables & timeline
  • Fixed price agreed before work begins
  • Milestone-based payment schedule
  • Ideal for MVPs, websites & one-off integrations
✦ Best for: Startups & one-off projects
Most Popular
👥

Dedicated Resource / Team

Your own offshore developer or full team. Flexible ramp-up/down, working in your timezone with daily standups.

  • Flexible ramp-up and ramp-down
  • UK / IST timezone overlap
  • Daily standups & code repo access
  • Scale without the hiring overhead
✦ Best for: Product companies & scale-ups
🤝

Support & Consulting Retainer

A monthly block of hours — use as needed for IT advisory, ongoing support, or small change requests.

  • Monthly block of hours, use flexibly
  • Priority response SLA guaranteed
  • Partial roll-over of unused hours
  • IT advisory included in every retainer
✦ Best for: SMEs needing ongoing IT support
